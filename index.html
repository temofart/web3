    – More detail on life coaching –

    Life coaching is not counseling and it's not therapy. Usually done by phone, life coaching is a practical, forward-looking way to gain clarity on what you don't like and what you can do about it. It's also a powerful support system to keep you moving in the right direction. It provides an external perspective on your life.
    1. We'll help you design a plan to achieve your life goals.
    2. We'll work with you to eliminate any obstacles that stand on your way.
    3. We'll partner with you all the way to success.
    4. We'll celebrate with you!

    Why does life coaching work so well?

    1. You'll take time out (get off the treadmill) to look at your life
    2. We'll learn exactly why your life is not working the way you want it to
    3. We'll clarify what you actually do want from your life
    4. We'll design inspiring yet believable goals for you
    1. We will find & remove your hidden beliefs - these drive your self-limiting behaviour
    2. Our sessions will help you stay focused, positive and on track - fighting those distractions
    3. You are always in control of the coaching process - we're here to help - but you are in charge
